About Us

Learn more about us and what we do!

JerryChris Inter Garment Ltd.

We're a privately owned garment design firm registered with Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission RC-960763.

We specializes in the design and production of polo, t-shirts, school sports wears of all designs, NYSC wears, graduation gowns, sweater, jackets, hand gloves, rain coat, ties, pants, caps, baby wears, and general goods supplier.

Our designs are specifically designed and produced by our in-house experts using the latest quality and technology while obeying the clients choice of work.

Beyond the design and production of these materials, we ensure direct delivery of products to clients with optimum customer satisfaction.
